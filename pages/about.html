Antonios Francesco Takos

Hello there! My name is Antonios, but most people call me Adoni.
I was born and raised in Queens, from a family of Greek and Italian backgrounds.

When I was a kid, I always loved playing video games, and when I was 13 years old, I started to play games that were on PC (ex: Team Fortress 2, Minecraft, Half-Life, etc.). However, my old ASUS laptop with the integrated graphics of its Intel Core i3 processor was holding me back from playing other titles that were more resource intensive.
Therefore, I learned how different computer components function and how to put them together to build a desktop gaming PC, which I succeeded in doing when I was 15 years old.

As I was learning how to do this, I came across what I considered to be enlightening and entertaining videos on technology overall. A couple of years later, it was time to start thinking about colleges and with that what kind of area I desired to study. The answer to me was clear that Computer Science would be the perfect choice!

Outside of my passion for technology, I have a strong devotion to Scouting. At the age of 18, I earned the rank of Eagle Scout, and I currently volunteer for my troop as an Assistant Scoutmaster to help guide others towards this same path.

Education:  St. John's University (Queens Campus)

In June of 2019, I graduated from St. Demetrios Astoria with an Advanced Regents Diploma. I am currenty a Senior at St. John's University studying for an ABET-accredited Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science (without a concentration). In addition to my academic career here at St. John's, I am also the Secretary for the university's Hellenic Society and an Undergraduate Research Assistant for one of my professors.
